<h1>Rock-Paper-Scissors</h1>

<h2>How to play</h2>
<p>Simply input rock, paper, or scissors when the text prompt "Rock, Paper, Scissors!" is on screen. The game ends when the player or the computer gets to five points. It's rock, paper, scissors..it's not hard</p>

<h2>How to tell who's winning</h3>
<p>Open the DevTools console by pressing the <code>F12</code> button on your keyboard or your preferred method of opening DevTools. On the console you will be told what the computer chose, what you chose, who won the round and the current score.</p>
